The idea behind this website is to attract lucrative business owners to the Everett area. We're hoping that they set up their businesses in the area and join the Chamber of Commerce. We're also looking to attact investors. Obviously, there's going to be many small, mom and pop type shops, and we're all about supporting the local community, what better way to support than connect their potential investors to them.

Persona 1: Johnathan Major

Motivation: The opportunity of investing in a local business. Looking for: A solid business, with a good foundation to invest into and get a solid ROI out of. Looking to do: Johnathan is looking to support a business in his local community and in turn accumulate more personal wealth. This for him is clearly a joint venture opportunity. He wants to have his hand in the pie of something much larger than himself. Needs: He needs to trust this business. He needs to know that his money isn't going to be wasted and that he's not going to be taken for granted. He needs a reputable business with a history of doing quality work, and has solid quantitative results.

Persona 2: The Big Bear Bakery

Motivation: Increase their reach and build a positive reputation in the community. Looking For: A platform to share what their about and a local fanbase to support them. Looking To: expand business operations both in the local area and hopefully in other parts of the nation. Needs: A backed collective of similarly minded individuals with goals that align with their own.
